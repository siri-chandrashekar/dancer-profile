Choreography

Creating movement that speaks to the soul

Choreography in Bharatanatyam is the art of weaving together movement, music, and meaning into a cohesive narrative. Each piece I create draws from classical compositions while incorporating personal interpretation and contemporary relevance. My choreographic work explores themes ranging from ancient mythology to modern social issues, always maintaining the sacred essence of this traditional art form while making it accessible to diverse audiences.

My Creative Process

1
Inspiration & Research

Every choreographic work begins with deep research into the chosen theme, whether it's a classical composition, mythological story, or contemporary concept. I study the musical structure, lyrical content, and cultural context to understand the essence of what I want to convey.

2
Movement Exploration

I experiment with traditional adavus and expressions, finding new ways to interpret familiar movements. This phase involves countless hours of practice, exploring how each gesture can contribute to the overall narrative and emotional arc of the piece.

3
Refinement & Integration

The final stage involves refining the choreography, ensuring seamless transitions between movements, and integrating all elements - music, costume, lighting, and space - to create a cohesive artistic experience that honors tradition while speaking to contemporary audiences.
