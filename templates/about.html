Siri Chandrashekar is a passionate multifaceted artist hailing from Bangalore, currently based in Alabama, USA.

Bharatanatyam

From the early age of ten, Siri started learning Indian Classical artform, Bharatanatyam, under the tutelage of Guru Smt. Brunda Srinivas from Davangere. At the age of twelve, she moved to Bangalore and since then she has been continuing her journey of dance under the able guidance of Sri P. Praveen Kumar at Chithkala School of Dance, Bangalore. Through rigorous training under her Guru, she emerged as a full-fledged dancer, winning many competitions, awards, and accolades throughout her dance career. Siri did her Arangetrum (Rangapravesha) in 2016 at ADA Rangamandira, Bangalore and was well appreciated by esteemed guests and art connoisseurs. Siri, as a soloist, has showcased her talent extensively within India and abroad like Singapore and USA. She has had the privilege to perform with her Guru, Sri P. Praveen Kumar, in multiple performances held in Turkmenistan. Her performances have been critically acclaimed in magazines and are recognized for leaving a lasting impression on the audience, leaving them eager for more. Currently she is actively performing and collaborating with artists from various disciplines in USA and India. The influence of her Guru and her passionate love for dance have inspired Siri to aim towards spreading the joy of Indian Classical Art to all generations across the globe.
